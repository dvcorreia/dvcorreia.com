A guy that loves sound and studied electronics and telecommunications engineering because he wanted to build guitar pedal. 

Halfway into the college he got frustrated with building electronic projects that only provided a terminal interface to interact with, so he started learning web development. At that time, he got deep into programming and designing systems, so eventually, in the last year of college, went abroad to study as an exchange student at [Politecnico di Torino](https://www.polito.it) in Turin, Italy. There he got to work with a lot of cool things: machine learning, distributed systems, cloud and automotive systems.

Then he joined [Wavecom](https://wavecom.com), which really liked the work I did during my master thesis, and for the last 3 years he has been working on real-time location systems, written in go and python, for manufacturing industries and healthcare sectors.

He's a cloud native fan that is now really into [Nix](https://nixos.org/).
In hes free time, he like to read technical blogs, watch conference talks, play instruments and experiment with crazy ideas.
