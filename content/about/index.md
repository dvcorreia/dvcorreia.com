## Work

I work as a software engineer. You can learn more about my professional experience on my [resumé]({{% relref "/resume" %}}).

I'm a generalist programmer.
From putting [WebAssembly](https://webassembly.org/) in microcontrollers, working on neuroscience browser applications, writing a Bluetooth Low Energy firewall, to deploying policy engines to Kubernetes, I've loved working through it all.

I've been a bit self-conscious about this, but I'm starting to feel comfortable with it.
The fallacy is that a generalist is not a master of anything.
I believe that with great commitment, being a generalist is being fluent on many things.
It sharpens your understanding on what is possible and how it can be done.
