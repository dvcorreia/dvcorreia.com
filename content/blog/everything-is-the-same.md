Capitalism, Communism are not faroff. This one is not that hard to prove, but
none is ever without the other.
Whenever you see capitalism, you see communism.
They really don't mean much in practice.
If I've learned anything from working these last 2 years, the most clear think I burned inside my head is that, good ideas don't mean shit compared to good implementation.
Ideas do not bring value, implementation do.
Ideas by themselves,are just ideas, which by their definition are ideias: a thought or suggestion as to a possible course of action. 
They live in the heads of people.
Sometimes you enact an *implementation* of an *idea*, which will turn out different from your initial *idea*, which sometimes enact an *implemen*... and on and on.

So implementations are what matter.
Great implementations of great ideias.
